Situated right beside the Cupertino Library, Coffee Society is a capsule of all your cafe needs. Enjoy the lovely ambience of our indoor/outdoor garden as you wait for your order, whether it be our coffee, milk teas, or smoothies.

If the day is calling for something sweet, then be sure to get one of our Marianne's ice cream scopes as our flavors rotate seasonally. Not sure what to get? Our baristas would love to share with you our top flavors or their personal favorites.
